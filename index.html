Core Features

Purpose-built automation for marine layouts and permit coordination.

Marine Plan AI is structured around the actual handoff points that slow waterfront projects: code interpretation, facility geometry, environmental packet assembly, and dock scheduling.

01

Multi-state compliance

Generate outputs aligned to launch-state requirements including Michigan, Oregon, and future state profiles as coverage expands.

  • State-specific output modes for active jurisdictions.
  • Consistent document structure across expanding coverage.
  • Supports teams managing projects across multiple regions.
02

Marina layout generation

Convert facility inputs into dock, slip, and circulation layouts designed for review-ready planning packages.

  • Slip counts, geometry, and berth allocation surfaced together.
  • Utility and access planning included in the same workflow.
  • Reduces redraw cycles between concept and submittal packages.
03

Environmental permit documents

Produce supporting documentation cues and structured permit packets that account for environmental review steps.

  • Organizes supporting materials into a single delivery path.
  • Links layout decisions with documentation requirements.
  • Improves internal coordination before submission.
04

Dock & slip scheduling

Keep berth planning, dock sequencing, and operational turnover schedules aligned with the generated facility layout.

  • Scheduling outputs tied directly to generated plan geometry.
  • Supports phased build-outs and operations planning.
  • Helps teams communicate capacity and sequencing faster.
