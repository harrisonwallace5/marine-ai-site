Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Marine Plan AI handles vessel plans, marina facility drawings, and related contact details submitted for internal schematic generation.

Effective Date: June 2025

What We Collect

We collect vessel plans, marina facility drawings, and the name, email address, and company name submitted with demo requests.

How We Use It

We use submitted materials to generate marine piping schematics through internal AI processing and deliver results by email.

Data Retention

Uploaded plans and related drawings are deleted within 24 hours after delivery. Contact information is retained only for necessary follow-up unless you request opt-out.

No Sale of Data

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for their marketing use.

No Direct Model Access

There is no public API or direct user access to the model. Marine Plan AI operates as a demo-only intake and email-delivery workflow.

Security

We maintain reasonable technical and organizational measures intended to protect uploaded plans and personal information.

CCPA Notice

California residents have rights to know, delete, and opt out of sale of personal information. We do not sell data.

GDPR Notice

EU and UK users may request access, rectification, erasure, or portability of their personal data, subject to applicable law.

Governing Law

This policy is governed by Texas law, and any disputes shall be resolved in Travis County, Texas.
